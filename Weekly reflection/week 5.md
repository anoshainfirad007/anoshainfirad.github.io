## 📘 Week 1: Codes of Conduct

### 📖 Topic Covered:
This week introduced the fundamental concept of codes of conduct in the computing profession, focusing on their role in guiding ethical and responsible professional behavior.
### 🧠 What I Learned:
I learned that a **code of conduct** is a set of ethical principles, rules, and standards that guide professionals in their work and behavior.

These codes ensure that decisions made in the computing field are ethical, fair, responsible, and focused on the public good. They are important because computing systems have a major impact on society.

Without these guidelines, decisions could prioritize profit or convenience over human rights, privacy, and well-being.

### 💡 Key Insight:
I understood that professional codes of conduct are not strict rules to be followed blindly. Instead, they act as **guiding principles** that help professionals make the right decisions in complex situations.

### 🌍 Real-life Application:
In real-life situations, such as being asked to collect user data without proper consent, a professional should follow ethical principles by prioritizing user privacy and ensuring informed consent is obtained.

Similarly, in engineering or development work, public safety should always be the highest priority, even if it conflicts with deadlines or management pressure.
